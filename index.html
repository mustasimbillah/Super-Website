I AM IROMMAN

You’ve seen the suit, the tech, the fireworks… but let’s make one thing clear, Iron Man isn’t just the armor. It’s the mind inside it. The man who refuses to sit back and watch the world burn.

I’m not a soldier, I’m not a god, I’m not a perfect hero. I’m a man who built his own way out of the fire and decided to use that fire to protect others. Every circuit, every bolt, every insane upgrade you’ve seen? I built it, not because I had to, but because I chose to.

I’ve made mistakes, I’ve faced enemies that should’ve crushed me, and I’ve stood in front of storms no one else could survive. And you know what? I’m still here.
